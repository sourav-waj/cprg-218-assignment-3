A Culinary Journey

Indulge in a diverse range of dining experiences at Shangri La Beach Resort. From elegant fine dining to casual beachfront bites, our restaurants and bars offer something to tantalize every taste bud and create unforgettable culinary moments.

The Beach House

Fine Dining with Ocean Views

Experience the epitome of culinary excellence at our signature restaurant, offering panoramic ocean views. Enjoy exquisite dishes crafted with fresh seafood and premium ingredients in an elegant, romantic ambiance.

  • Cuisine: Fine Dining, Seafood, International
  • Ambiance: Elegant, Romantic, Oceanfront
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Reservations: Recommended

The Sand Bar

Relaxed Beachfront Grill

This vibrant beachfront grill offers a laid-back atmosphere where you can enjoy grilled specialties, fresh salads, and casual bites with your feet in the sand—perfect for lunch or dinner under the stars.

  • Cuisine: Casual, Grill, Seafood, American
  • Ambiance: Relaxed, Beachfront, Informal
  • Dress Code: Casual
  • Reservations: Not Required

The Poolside Cafe

Refreshments and Light Bites

Grab a quick bite or chilled drink by the pool. From fresh wraps and snacks to tropical cocktails and juices, The Poolside Cafe keeps you refreshed without leaving the water’s edge.

  • Cuisine: Casual, Light Bites, Snacks, Beverages
  • Ambiance: Relaxed, Poolside, Informal
  • Dress Code: Casual
  • Reservations: Not Required

The Lobby Lounge

Sophisticated Cocktails and Ambiance

Enjoy handcrafted cocktails, fine wines, and light appetizers in an elegant lounge atmosphere. Perfect for a pre-dinner drink, a nightcap, or relaxing with live music in the evenings.

  • Cuisine: Beverages, Light Appetizers
  • Ambiance: Sophisticated, Relaxed, Live Music (evenings)
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Reservations: Not Required

Other Dining Options

  • Room Service: Extensive 24-hour in-room dining menu available for your convenience and privacy.
  • Buffet Breakfast: A luxurious spread of hot & cold dishes, fruits, pastries, and more each morning.
  • Special Dietary Options: We accommodate allergies and dietary needs—just let us know.
